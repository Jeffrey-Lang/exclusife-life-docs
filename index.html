Get started

Exclusife life has a few basic rules in the city, make sure you follow them to avoid getting kicked or banned. Also be sure to report any cheaters or exploiders. For example if you get alot of money from a cheater and we see that you have alot of money you are the one seen as a cheater and will get banned so if you do get alot of money from a hacker report it. here are the rules:

  • Roleplay only
  • No stealing of EMS/Police/mechicanic vehicles
  • No cop baiting
  • No RDM or VDM
  • When you die, you lost memory of how and who killed you, you will start a new life.
  • Voice chat is required
  • Follow the law

Those are the rules if you do not follow these rules you risk a ban/kick.
